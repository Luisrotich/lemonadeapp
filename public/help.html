Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, enter your email address, create a password, and follow the verification steps. You'll be ready to start in just 2 minutes.
Is Lemonade App free to use?
Yes! Lemonade App offers a completely free basic plan with all essential features. We also offer premium plans with additional features for power users and businesses.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link will expire in 24 hours for security.
Can I use Lemonade App on multiple devices?
Absolutely! Your Lemonade account syncs across all your devices. Simply download the app from your device's app store or access via web browser.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Account Settings > Subscription, and click "Cancel Subscription." Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
